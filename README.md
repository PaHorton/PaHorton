# Paul Horton — Exploration Systems Design, ASU

Hi — I'm Paul. I design systems for exploring inhospitable places (space-adjacent work), build small data tooling, and occasionally teach computers to read messy handwriting. I hold a PhD in Exploration Systems Design from Arizona State University, where my research focused on the "Development, Implementation, and Impacts of Novelty Detection Systems for Mission Operations." This involved identifying anomalies in large-scale datasets from missions like the Mars Curiosity rover (Mastcam), the Roman Space Telescope (WFI), and the ASTHROS stratospheric balloon observatory. I also serve as a Coach at Major League Hacking.
